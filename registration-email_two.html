Award Categories
Awards (Academic performance in Hindi):
Qualification Criteria:
CBSE X & CBSE XII – 70 marks in Hindi
ICSE & ISC - 75 marks in Hindi
WBBSE & WBCHSE– 65 marks in Hindi
Selection Criteria:
50% weightage on board marks
25% weightage on pre-board marks
25% weightage on all-year performance over the last two years
Winners:
1. Topper - 4 Toppers (2 girls and 2 boys) from each board and class
2. Top 10 Awards: The next top 10 students from each board and class
3. Certificate Holders: All the remaining students who meet the qualification criteria
ANKUR Awards (Extracurricular Activities):
Criteria:
40% weightage on board marks
60% weightage on extracurricular activities
Winners:
6 students awarded based on their extracurricular activities in Hindi
APARAJAY Awards (Specially abled):
Criteria:
Hindi marks above 50
Suffering from physical/mental challenges during their board exams
Winners:
Maximum 3 students to be selected by the jury
HINDI MEDIUM TOPPER Awards:
Winners: 3 toppers each from class 10 & 12 from Hindi Medium Schools
